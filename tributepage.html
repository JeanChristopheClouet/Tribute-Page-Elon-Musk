Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a businessman who is currently the second richest man of the world, with a net worth peaking at 171.8 Billion dollars. He is the founder of the private rocket company SpaceX and owns a bunch of other successful companies.

About the man

  • Early Life: Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He showed an early aptitude for science and technology, and by the age of 12, he had already taught himself computer programming.
  • Education: Musk moved to the United States at the age of 17 to attend college. He attended the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned dual degrees in Physics and Economics.
  • Business Ventures: Musk co-founded a web software company called Zip2 in 1995, which he sold for over $300 million. He then founded X.com, an online payment company, which later became PayPal. PayPal was sold to eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002.
  • SpaceX: In 2002, Musk founded SpaceX, a private space exploration company. SpaceX has made history by being the first privately-funded company to send a spacecraft to the International Space Station and land reusable rockets.
  • Tesla: In 2004, Musk joined the board of Tesla Motors, an electric car company. He later became CEO, and under his leadership, the company has become a major player in the automotive industry.
  • SolarCity: In 2006, Musk co-founded SolarCity, a solar panel installation company. The company was later acquired by Tesla.
  • Hyperloop: In 2013, Musk announced plans for a high-speed transportation system called the Hyperloop, which would use vacuum-sealed tubes to transport passengers at speeds of up to 700 mph.
  • Personal Life: Musk has been married three times and has six children. He has also been known for his outspoken views on a variety of topics, including artificial intelligence and renewable energy.
